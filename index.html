Revision Notes

In a chat with my girlfriend, when she asks me about why I spend time answering questions about things learnt in class, and I said, "Well, although I only belong to you, knowledge shall belong to everyone." Indeed this is my heartfelt revelation about knowledge.

Knowledge is empowering in many ways. However, I, as one active learner, feel more humbled than empowered in learning.

The more I learn, the more I realize how much I don't know.

This is exactly what I feel when encountering new things.

Apart from the mistakes I commit in learning, my memory also fails me as I tend to forget about what I have grasped. Therefore, I decided to make this series of Revision Notes since A level, as a personal reference.

I realise that these revision notes may be beneficial for people taking the same module as I did. So I decided to make it open-source for public consumption today, 29 September 2017.

Happy reading and happy learning!
